FAQ

What is HerBench?

HerBench is an AI companion built specifically for women managing the dual weight of home and professional life. She handles the logistics, coordination, and mental overhead so you can focus on what actually requires you.

How is this different from a regular AI assistant?

HerBench isn't a general-purpose chatbot. She's built with a specific emotional tone, a specific kind of support posture, and a deep understanding of the invisible load women carry. The difference shows up in how she communicates, not just what she does.
