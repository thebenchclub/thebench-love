The SMB Bench is an AI automation service for small business owners who are ready to stop spending 15–20 hours a week on admin. We build the systems — you run the business.

Service-based small businesses of all kinds — tutoring practices, wellness studios, coaching programs, consultancies, agencies, and more. If administrative complexity is standing between you and growth, the SMB Bench was made for you.

We work with a range of AI and automation tools, but we always choose based on your business model, budget, and comfort level. Wherever possible, we build on tools you already use.

Most clients feel the impact within the first week. The first automation that runs while you're doing something else is usually the moment it clicks.
