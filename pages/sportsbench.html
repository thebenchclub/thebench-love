Common questions

FAQ

What is the Sports Bench?

The Sports Bench is an AI operations system for sports organizations, athletic directors, coaches, and team managers who are spending too much time on communications, scheduling, and administrative logistics.

What sports and levels does this work for?

Any sport, any level — youth leagues, high school programs, club teams, collegiate organizations. If you're managing a team and drowning in ops, the Sports Bench is built for you.

Do parents need to download anything?

No. Communications go out via email or SMS — no new apps, no new accounts for parents or players.

How long does setup take?

Typically 2–5 days from your first conversation to your first automations running. Most organizations see the impact within the first week.
